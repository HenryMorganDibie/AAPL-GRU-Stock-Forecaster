## 1. Final Evaluation Metrics (MAPE)

The Mean Absolute Percentage Error (MAPE) scores on the validation set demonstrate the model's robustness:

| Horizon | MAPE Score | Performance Interpretation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1 Day Ahead** | **0.2726%** | Outstanding: The model's prediction is, on average, less than 0.3% off the actual price. |

| **1 Month Ahead (21 Days)** | **4.1789%** | Strong Performance: Maintaining an error under 5% across a full month of market volatility is a significant achievement. |

*These results validate the project's methodology, showing that the non-linear capabilities of the GRU network were well-suited to model the non-stationary, trend-heavy nature of the stock market time series.*

---

## 2. Model Selection Rationale

Both LSTM and GRU architectures were considered (saved models are available in `models/`). The GRU was chosen for these reasons:

* **Efficiency:** GRU has fewer parameters, leading to faster training and reduced computational load.
* **Simplicity:** Simpler architecture reduces the risk of overfitting compared to LSTM, resulting in a more stable production model.
